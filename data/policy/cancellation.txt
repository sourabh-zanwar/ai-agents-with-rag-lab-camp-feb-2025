# Cancellation Policy

Cancellation charges depend on ticket class and the time of cancellation.

## Business Class Tickets:

Fully refundable if canceled at least 48 hours before departure.

A 10% cancellation fee applies for cancellations within 48 hours.

No refund for no-shows.

## Flexi Tickets:

Fully refundable if canceled at least 72 hours before departure.

A 20% cancellation fee applies within 72 hours.

No refund if canceled within 24 hours of departure.

## Economy Tickets:

50% refund if canceled at least 7 days before departure.

25% refund if canceled between 7 days and 24 hours before departure.

No refund for cancellations within 24 hours.
